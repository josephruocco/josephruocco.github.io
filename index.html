Good Habits Are...

  • Small: Focus on one easy, tiny action. Do less than you can.
  • Triggerable: No thinking required to start. Doing it every day is natural and easy.
  • Rewarding: It's competitive, or it's something you fight to keep.
  • Irreversible: You get rid of old ways. Change your environment so you have to accept the new behavior.
  • Daily: You do it everyday.
  • Effective: You can measure your progress, and you can improve with mentors.
