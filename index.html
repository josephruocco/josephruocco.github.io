About

First, I was lazy and fat. Then, the second day, I was not lazy and fat. Then I was not lazy, and not fat. Today, I am lazy, not fat.

I have over 100 daily “set & forget” healthy habits that you can start in 5 minutes and do everyday with minimal effort.

For the last 5 years, I have spent collecting easy habits backed by scientific research to create for myself.

The result is an easy acronym for how good habits are made with STRIDE: Small, Triggerable, Rewarding, Irreversible, Daily, Effective.

This book will teach you this method, and give you enough habits to get started on your own.
