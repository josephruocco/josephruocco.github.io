Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

What AntiDoom does

AntiDoom is a Chrome extension that detects excessive scrolling on distracting websites and displays a motivational popup to encourage you to stop and take a break.

What data we collect

None. AntiDoom does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

All settings (scroll thresholds, enabled sites, popup interval) are stored locally on your device using Chrome's chrome.storage.sync API. This data syncs across your Chrome instances via your Google account — it is never sent to or accessible by us.

Permissions we use and why

PermissionWhy
storageSave your settings locally
activeTabDetect which site you're on to decide whether to activate
scriptingInject the scroll-detection and popup scripts
tabsClose the educational redirect tab after it opens
Host permissionsOnly activate on the specific sites you've enabled (Reddit, YouTube, etc.)

Third-party services

None. AntiDoom makes no external network requests.
