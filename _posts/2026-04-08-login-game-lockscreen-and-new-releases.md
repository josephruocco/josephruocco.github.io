I'm trying something new - posting daily about what I'm working on, ideas I'm having, where my head's at. Not sure if I'll stick with it but figured I'd start and see.

## Login Game

A while back I made a [login game](https://x.com/JoeRuocco/status/1831762254527869239/video/1) - a login page where you had to figure out how to actually log in. Last night I came back to it with a new version: a page full of fake Login buttons scattered everywhere. You have to find the real one.

![Login game screenshot](/assets/posts/login-game-screenshot.png)

Still a simple concept but I think there's something fun here. The login screen is a moment everyone interacts with and no one thinks about.

## Lockscreen Game

This idea was randomly spurred from a random piece of internet humor I remember seeing ages ago.

![iPad disabled for 25 million minutes](/assets/posts/ipad-disabled.webp)

Something about the absurdity of that got me thinking: what if the lockscreen itself was the game? Instead of a passcode or a swipe, you'd play a small game to unlock your phone. But careful - guess wrong and you'll get disabled.
Still early on this one but the idea has legs.
